Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-PUD: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

2671 tokens (14%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 1520 types (20%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 943 lemmas (18%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (1755; 9% instances), AUX (648; 3% instances), ADJ (268; 1% instances).
