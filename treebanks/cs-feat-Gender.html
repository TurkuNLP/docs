Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech: Features: Gender

This feature is universal. It occurs with 3 different values: Fem, Masc, Neut. Some words have combined values of the feature; 3 combinations have been observed: Fem|Masc, Fem|Neut, Masc|Neut.

This is a layered feature with the following layers: Gender, Gender[psor].

753255 tokens (50%) have a non-empty value of Gender. 122285 types (95%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. 49988 lemmas (86%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of Gender. The feature is used with 8 part-of-speech tags: NOUN (371970; 25% instances), ADJ (185716; 12% instances), PROPN (82083; 5% instances), VERB (50342; 3% instances), DET (43675; 3% instances), PRON (7931; 1% instances), AUX (6779; 0% instances), NUM (4759; 0% instances).
