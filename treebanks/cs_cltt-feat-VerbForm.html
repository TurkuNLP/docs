Treebank Statistics: UD_Czech-CLTT: Features: VerbForm

This feature is universal but the values Conv are language-specific. It occurs with 4 different values: Conv, Fin, Inf, Part.

2975 tokens (8%) have a non-empty value of VerbForm. 756 types (17%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. 366 lemmas (14%) occur at least once with a non-empty value of VerbForm. The feature is used with 3 part-of-speech tags: VERB (2117; 6% instances), AUX (570; 2% instances), ADJ (288; 1% instances).
